Here is a collection of other helpful resources for beginner and veteran players alike.

    A subreddit designed to help players old and new figure out their next character, understand the game more and answer any other questions that players may have.

  • Chicken Dinner

    Chicken Dinner is a point buy calculator for D&D 5e.

    A collection of official Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition resources from Wizards of the Coast in one place

  • Basic Rules

    The official basic rules you need to start playing D&D.
